# Minerlytics

Minerlytics is a web application that empowers retail investors to **discover, evaluate, and monitor commodity mining opportunities** using **AI-driven insights**.

TEST LINK: https://minerlytics.pages.dev

The platform combines **multi-source data ingestion**, **machine learning**, and **interactive analytics** to deliver forward-looking investment signals, risk assessments, scenario simulations, and diligence support for mining and commodity-focused equities.

## Vision

Minerlytics aims to reduce information asymmetry in commodity investing by unifying **market data, mine fundamentals, regulatory context, and sentiment intelligence** into a single, intuitive platform.

---

## What Minerlytics Enables

- Faster, higher-confidence investment screening
- AI-assisted due diligence for mining equities
- Quantified regulatory, ESG, and geopolitical risk
- Scenario simulations based on commodity price movements
- Sentiment and momentum tracking across media and interviews

---

## Sentiment & Momentum Intelligence

### Sentiment
- Automatically scores content as positive, neutral, or negative
- Aggregates sentiment across time, assets, and topics
- Identifies improving or deteriorating perception

### Momentum
- Detects acceleration in price, volume, and media exposure
- Flags early-stage opportunities
- Differentiates hype vs data-backed momentum
