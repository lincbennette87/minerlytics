About Minerlytics

Minerlytics AI provides retail investors with an accessible, research-grade AI platform that empowers deep, data-driven analysis of miners and commodities so they can make informed investment decisions with confidence. We enable retail investors of mining and precious-metals who want faster signal discovery and cleaner decision support. We bring together market data, thematic watchlists, and structured workflows so you can track what’s moving miners — without juggling ten different tabs.

What we do
  • Track mining equities, ETFs, and commodities with momentum-style views.
  • Translate complex mining and performance data to clear information to support decision making.
  • Highlight “what changed” across recent windows to spot inflections early.
  • Organize tickers by themes (gold, silver, copper, developers, producers, explorers).
  • Turn raw data into quick, readable insights for daily decision-making.
Our approach
  • Signal-first: prioritize actionable deltas over noisy headlines.
  • Explainable: keep outputs transparent and easy to sanity-check.
  • Research-grade: design for repeatable workflows and auditability.
  • Investor-focused: insights built for real portfolio decisions.
Minerlytics AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is designed to help you query the platform’s stored market dataset (e.g., OHLCV time series) in plain English — things like “summarize the last 30 days” or “show the biggest drawdowns.” It is intended for research and education, not financial advice.
