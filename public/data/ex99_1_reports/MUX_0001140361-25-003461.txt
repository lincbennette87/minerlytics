 Select Estimated Preliminary Unaudited Financial Information as of December 31, 2024

 “Risk Factors” and “Cautionary Note Regarding Forward-Looking Statements” for additional information regarding factors that could result in differences between our preliminary estimated year-end cash balance and the actual year-end cash balance and
