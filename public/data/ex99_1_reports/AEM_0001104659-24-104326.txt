AGNICO EAGLE PROVIDES NOTICE OF RELEASE OF

THIRD QUARTER 2024 RESULTS AND CONFERENCE CALL

 

Toronto (September 25, 2024) – Agnico
Eagle Mines Limited (NYSE: AEM, TSX: AEM) ("Agnico Eagle" or the "Company") today announced that
it will release its third quarter 2024 results on Wednesday, October 30, 2024, after normal trading hours.

 

Third Quarter 2024 Results Conference Call
and Webcast

 

Agnico Eagle's senior management will host a conference
call on Thursday, October 31, 2024, at 11:00 AM (E.D.T.) to discuss the Company's financial and operating results.

 

Via Webcast:

 

To listen to the live webcast of the conference
call, you may register on the Company website at www.agnicoeagle.com, or directly via the link here.

416.945.7677 or toll-free 1.888.699.1199 to be entered into the call by an operator. To ensure your participation, please call approximately

About Agnico Eagle

 

Agnico Eagle is a Canadian based and led senior
gold mining company and the third largest gold producer in the world, producing precious metals from operations in Canada, Australia,
Finland and Mexico. It has a pipeline of high-quality exploration and development projects in these countries as well as in the United
States. Agnico Eagle is a partner of choice within the mining industry, recognized globally for its leading environmental, social and
governance practices. Agnico Eagle was founded in 1957 and has consistently created value for its shareholders, declaring a cash dividend
every year since 1983.
