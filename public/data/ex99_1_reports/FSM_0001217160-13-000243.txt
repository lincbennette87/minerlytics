NEWS RELEASE DATED AUGUST 7, 2013

Fortuna News Release

Fortuna Silver to release second quarter financial results on August 12, 2013; Conference call at 12 p.m. Eastern on August 13, 2013

Vancouver, August 7, 2013-- Fortuna Silver Mines Inc. (NYSE: FSM | TSX: FVI | BVL: FVI | Frankfurt: F4S.F) is pleased to announce that it will release its financial statements and MD&A for the second quarter on Monday, August 12, 2013.

A conference call to discuss the financial and operations results will be held on Tuesday, August 13, 2013 at 9:00 a.m. Pacific | 11:00 a.m. Lima | 12:00 p.m. Eastern. Hosting the call will be Jorge A. Ganoza, President and CEO and Luis D. Ganoza, Chief Financial Officer. 

Shareholders, analysts, media and interested investors are invited to listen to the live conference call by logging onto the webcast at: http://www.investorcalendar.com/IC/CEPage.asp?ID=171413 or over the phone by dialing just prior to the starting time.

Fortuna Silver Mines Inc.

Fortuna is a growth oriented, silver and base metal producer focused on mining opportunities in Latin America. Our primary assets are the Caylloma silver mine in southern Peru and the San Jose silver-gold mine in Mexico. The company is selectively pursuing additional acquisition opportunities throughout the Americas. For more information, please visit our website at www.fortunasilver.com.
