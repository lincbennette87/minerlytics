“Additional Shares”) at a price of $4.50 per Additional Share for the purposes of covering over-allotments and for market stabilization purposes. The Over-Allotment Option may be exercised in accordance with Section 15 hereof.
The Firm Shares and the Additional Shares are collectively referred to herein as the “Offered Shares”.
