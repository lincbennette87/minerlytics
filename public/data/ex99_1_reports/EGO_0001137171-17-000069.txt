About Eldorado Gold

Eldorado is a leading intermediate gold producer
with mining, development and exploration operations in Turkey, Greece, Romania, Serbia and Brazil. The Company’s success
to date is based on a highly skilled workforce, safe and responsible operations, a portfolio of high-quality assets, and long-term
partnerships with the communities where we operate. Eldorado’s common shares trade on the Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX: ELD)
and the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: EGO).
