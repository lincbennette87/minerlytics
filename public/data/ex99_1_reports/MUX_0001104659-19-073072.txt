ABOUT MCEWEN MINING

 

McEwen Mining is a diversified gold and
silver producer with operating mines in Nevada, Canada, Mexico and Argentina. It also owns a large copper deposit in Argentina.
McEwen’s goal is to create a profitable gold and silver producer focused in the Americas.

 

McEwen has approximately 400.3 million
shares outstanding. Rob McEwen, Chairman and Chief Owner, owns 20% of the shares.
