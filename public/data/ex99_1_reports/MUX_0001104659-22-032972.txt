McEwen Mining Provides 2022 Guidance

 

TORONTO,
March 10th, 2022 - McEwen Mining Inc. (NYSE: MUX) (TSX: MUX) is pleased to provide production and cost guidance
for 2022. We are estimating production of 153,000 to 172,000 gold equivalent ounces (GEOs) in 2022. Cash costs and all-in sustaining
costs (AISC) per GEO sold for 2022 from our 100%-owned mines (Gold Bar and Fox) are expected to be in the range $1,310-1,410 and
$1,570-1,690, respectively. At San José, production is estimated to be 69,500 to 77,500
GEOs at an AISC per GEO sold of $1,330-1,370.
