No. 1968/004880/06) (Incorporated in the Republic of South Africa) JSE, NYSE, DIFX Share Code: GFI ISIN Code: ZAE000018123 (“Gold Fields” or the “Company”) DEALINGS IN SECURITIES – ACCEPTANCE OF PERFORMANCE SHARE RIGHTS In compliance with paragraphs 3.63 to 3.74 of the Listings Requirements of JSE Limited (the “Listings Requirements”) the Company hereby advises that Executive Directors and Prescribed Officers of Gold Fields, Directors of major subsidiaries of the Company, and the Company Secretary of the Company, have accepted conditional Performance Share Rights (“PSR”) which were awarded in terms of the Gold Fields Share Incentive Plan 2025 (“Scheme”). The PSR are effective as at 1 March 2025 and have a three-year vesting period. The price per share for the purposes of determining the allocation of PSR was the 20-day VWAP from 3 February to 28 February 2025 as defined in the Scheme. Vesting of the PSR will be determined by the Company based on the prescribed performance conditions measured over a three-year performance period from 1 January 2025 to 31 December 2027.
